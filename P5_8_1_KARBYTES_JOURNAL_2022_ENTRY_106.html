The picture of the tattoo below appears (to me) to be suggesting that magic mushrooms are more useful for “leveling up” one’s introspective faculties than is cannabis. I find that claim to be true based on my own experiences. This is not to say that cannabis has not been a vital and useful part of my journey in becoming a more experienced enthenogenic plant and psychedelics user over the past six years. Cannabis was useful to me as a “gateway drug” but I find it to now be a bit too “low level” for the stage in psychonautical evolution which I am at. Still, I use it occasionally to feel uplifted (but at this time I like being clear headed, focused, and more abundant with attention span).

Aside: One thing that annoys me about how cannabis is portrayed in “the mainstream media” is how smoking the substance is significantly more emphasized than is eating it. (The former is carcinogenic (i.e. cancer causing) while the latter is claimed to be anti-carcinogenic (and the high from edibles is stronger and longer lasting)).
